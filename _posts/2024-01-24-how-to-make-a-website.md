### "Gerard"
Back in middle school - around 2006 - I was taught how to make a website. I remember our computer science teacher sitting us down, opening op notepad on the school's Windows XP computer and typing in our names, and saving the file as `index.htm`. We had to open it in Internet Explorer, and voila, there was your name in the browser. From that point he taught us what HTML is, what CSS is, and a bit of PHP and JavaScript made its way in, but it never became truly complicated. There was a certain novelty to it, a novelty I feel that's long gone.
